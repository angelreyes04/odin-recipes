Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a comforting and hearty Italian dish made with layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. The recipe begins with preparing a savory meat sauce, typically made with ground beef or sausage, sautéed with onions and garlic, and simmered in a flavorful tomato sauce seasoned with Italian herbs. A creamy layer of ricotta cheese, often mixed with eggs and Parmesan, adds a velvety texture to the dish. The lasagna is assembled by layering the cooked pasta sheets with alternating layers of meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese, then baked until bubbly and golden brown. The result is a deliciously satisfying dish, perfect for feeding a crowd or enjoying as leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups marinara or tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps to cook

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or sausage over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet, and sauté until the onion is soft and translucent.
  5. Stir in the marinara or tomato sauce, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper. Let the sauce simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  6. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, and egg. Mix until well combined.
  7. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  8. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  9. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  10. Sprinkle 1/3 of the shredded mozzarella cheese over the ricotta mixture.
  11. Repeat the layers: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese.
  12. Finish with a final layer of noodles, the remaining meat sauce, and the rest of the mozzarella cheese.
  13. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  14. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  15. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired.
