Korean Fried Chicken

Description

Korean Fried Chicken, known for its crispy texture and flavorful coating, is a popular dish that combines the crunch of fried chicken with a unique, tangy, and spicy glaze. The chicken is double-fried, which gives it an extra-crispy exterior while keeping the meat juicy and tender. After frying, the chicken is tossed in a sticky sauce made from a blend of gochujang (Korean chili paste), soy sauce, garlic, ginger, honey, and vinegar, creating a perfect balance of sweetness, heat, and umami. Often garnished with sesame seeds and served with pickled radishes or a cold beer, Korean Fried Chicken offers an irresistible combination of textures and flavors that make it a standout in the world of fried chicken dishes.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken wings or drumettes
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 cup cold water
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • 2 tablespoons gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • Sesame seeds for garnish (optional)
  • Chopped green onions for garnish (optional)

Steps to cook

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, cornstarch, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  2. Gradually add cold water to the dry ingredients, stirring until a smooth batter forms.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350째F (175째C).
  4. Dip each piece of chicken into the batter, ensuring it is fully coated.
  5. Carefully place the coated chicken pieces into the hot oil, frying in batches to avoid overcrowding.
  6. Fry the chicken for about 8-10 minutes until lightly golden, then remove and drain on a wire rack or paper towels.
  7. After all the chicken pieces have been fried once, increase the oil temperature to 375째F (190째C).
  8. Fry the chicken a second time for an additional 3-5 minutes until golden brown and extra crispy.
  9. While the chicken is frying, prepare the sauce by combining gochujang, soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, minced garlic, grated ginger, and sesame oil in a small saucepan over medium heat.
  10. Bring the sauce to a simmer, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly, then remove from heat.
  11. Toss the hot, double-fried chicken in the prepared sauce until evenly coated.
  12. Garnish the chicken with sesame seeds and chopped green onions if desired.
  13. Serve the Korean Fried Chicken immediately with pickled radishes or your favorite side dishes.
